This is a Floppa Site.
Floppa is a very cute cat with unique facial expressions that has brought joy to many people on the internet. This page is dedicated to Floppa.
Some information about our beloved Floppa.
Floppa's real name is Gosha. The meme community uses the name Floppa, in reference to the caracal's distinctive, large tufted ears.
Floppa's unique appearance and quirky personality have made him a beloved internet celebrity, with fans all over the world.
Floppa has inspired a wide range of merchandise, including clothing, mugs, phone cases, and even plush toys.
Floppa has a very playful personality and loves to chase after toys and play hide-and-seek with his owner.
